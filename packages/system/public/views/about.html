MY STORY

Growing up, I was extremely shy and introverted, I was always negative, lazy and I had list of friends that would agree in a heart beat, in turn I started suffering from depression and anxiety and I did not even know it. As you can imagine some of those habits carried over into college, and I continued to try to manage them as much as possible. It got so bad I felt that their was no way out of the deep dark cloud that surrounded me for years. Don't believe me? See why I am no longer employable, and how I ate my black heart out and most importantly find out how I managed to deal with those issues.

November 1, 2014 everything changed!

I looked myself in the mirror with a critical eye overweight, zits all over my face, broke with no money, overworked and nothing to show for it! I finally had enough. I reached the point of an "emotional threshold" and finally made a decision that would shape my life forever.

"I will NEVER settle for less than I can be, do, give, or create."

I knew that I was so much more than what I was demonstrating in life. I knew I had potential, and had finally decided to tap into it once an for all. With this in mind everything started turning around for me, I finally figured out what I am suppose to do, and the means of doing so. I created an awesome software application that let's you track your personal goals, stop relying on the university to provide me with education and started taking action on my own self education.

I quickly came to the conclusion that the only way to change myself would be to face my biggest fears that I have, and this is why this site was born.
